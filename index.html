About

Hello there! I'm Charles, a Frontend / Fullstack web developer based near Lagos. I wake up in the morning, grab a cu of timmies coffe, open my laptop and build things that live on the web all day, everyday!

People who know me say I'm obsessed, that's fair, I am very driven and love learning new things

Feel free to explore my GitHub profile, where you can find both completed projects and those I'm currently working on. I thrive in collaborative work environments, as they provide valuable learning experiences. I'm always eager to seize opportunities that allow me to further enhance my skills.
